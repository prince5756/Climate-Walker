My Achievements

From certifications to creating organizations, each achievement represents a step towards inspiring and empowering others through adventure and nature conservation.
    Diploma in TourismI pursued a diploma in hospitality, tour, and travel management from a wellknown institute in Surat, Gujarat, India.
    International Youth Festival – GreeceThe ‘Go Alive’ organization selected me for the youth festival in Greece to inspire Greek students and youth along with 25 other team members. They organized youth festivals in different parts of Greece and inspired thousands of people for non-formal learning.
    First Solo TravelerI am the first solo traveler from Gujarat who traveled all over India by walking and hitchhiking for 182 days. For this incredible journey of mine, I was featured on so many TV channels, magazines, and social media platforms.
    Certified Trekking InstructorI am a certifed trekking instructor and worked with India’s largest trekking organization, “Invincible NGO. ” Thousands of participants completed their dream treks under my guidance in different parts of India.
    Certified Kayaking InstructorIn 2022, I became a certied kayaking instructor. I passed India’s toughest institute for watersports “NIWS”s (National Institute of Watersports) exam and for that, I was honored with a license and certicate of kayaking.
    Certified Power ParagliderIn 2023, I successfully completed basic and advanced courses in power paragliding from a well-renowned institute called “NIMAS” and became a certied power paraglider
