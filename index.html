The Epic Journey

From the Mediterranean shores of Malta to the diverse landscapes of India, every step tells a story of climate resilience and hope.
  • About Me
    I am Jaydip from Bhavnagar, Gujarat, an adventurer and nature lover. I worked as a trekking instructor with Invincible NGO and explored India on foot for 182 days, which gave me a deeper purpose in life.
  • My Work
    I became a certified kayaking instructor and founded Gujarat’s first kayaking organization, where over 15,000 people trained. I am also a certified lifeguard and power paraglider, combining adventure with safety and skill.
  • Global Recognition
    My passion for travel and eco-tourism led me to study in Europe, and in 2025 I was invited to Greece for an international youth festival to inspire students and youth.

The Journey Chapters

617 Days. 18 Countries. One Planet. One Mission. Six distinct phases, each with unique challenges and discoveries.
  • Mediterranean Islands
    Starting in Malta's ancient harbors, walking through Italy's diverse landscapes to Vatican City's spiritual heart.
  • Balkan Crossroads
    Through Slovenia's Alps to Croatia's coast, across Bosnia's mountains and Serbia's plains.
  • Eastern Passage
    Bulgaria's rose valleys to Greece's ancient paths, crossing into Turkey's gateway between continents.
  • Caucasus Heights
    Georgia's wine country to Armenia's ancient monasteries, crossing some of Earth's highest mountain passes.
  • Persian Journey
    Iran's vast deserts and ancient cities, experiencing Persian hospitality and climate adaptation.
  • Himalayan Finale
    Pakistan's mighty peaks to India's diverse landscapes, completing the epic transcontinental journey.
