Walk for Earth: Malta to India

This solo walk from Malta to India, covering over 11,000 km, is a heartfelt mission to raise global awareness for climate change. With every footstep, I carry the spirit of the Walk for Earth, urging humanity to rethink its relationship with nature, reduce its ecological footprint, and act for a greener future. My journey is a call to protect what we all share: the Earth.

Distance & Duration

11,000 km on foot over several months, crossing continents and cultures to raise awareness for climate change.

Major Highlight

From the heart of Europe to the soul of South Asia, this journey isn't just a walk — it's a powerful call to action. Covering over 11,000 kilometers across 21 countries, this year-long expedition shines a spotlight on our shared climate challenges and the urgent need for sustainable solutions.

Walking through diverse ecosystems, cultures, and communities, the mission is to raise awareness, gather stories of resilience, and inspire change at every step — because the climate crisis knows no borders, and neither should our response.

Mission Statement

This solo walk under the Walk for Earth campaign is a reminder to act before it's too late. I will walk alone, but for the Earth and for every life it holds.
