My Story

The Journey of a Dreamer

Every great adventure begins with a single step. Here's how a brilliant student from Bhavnagar became a beacon of inspiration for thousands.

Humble Beginnings

Born in the small village of Bhavnagar, Gujarat, I was a brilliant student who initially pursued chartered accountancy (C.A.) in Surat. However, my heart was drawn to the vast possibilities of the tourism sector, leading me to pivot toward tour and travel management.

Finding My Path

After completing my management course, I became a certified trekking instructor and joined Invincible NGO, India's largest trekking organization. This marked the beginning of my journey as a senior trekking instructor and volunteer, where I discovered my true calling.

Life-Changing Journey

During COVID-19, I embarked on an incredible 182-day solo backpacking journey across India with almost no money. This transformative experience showed me the real and rural India, giving me practical knowledge about traveling and a deeper meaning in life.
